ganondorf

Prefered Stages

  • Avoid large stages

Strengths

  • Range [Smash Attacks Have A Surprising Long Range] (bait them then punish, do not airdodge near him)
  • Quick Tilts & Jab
  • Stomp [Dair] (mix up recovery)

Weaknesses

  • Recovery (don't get stage spiked by up b, be ready to tech)
  • Slow Movement
  • Combos
  • Projectiles
  • Camping

Notes

  • Has Command Grab
  • Can Suicide Kill
  • Neutral B Has Super Armour
